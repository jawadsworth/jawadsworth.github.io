About Us


Temple Inn & Suites is a hotel and hospitality chain established with the intent to make your future temple visits affordable and comfortable. Our mission is to serve you and attend to your needs while you serve others and attend the most memorable celebrations of your family and friends. We operate in locations near more than 50 temples of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. Each temple is either within walking distance of our properties or can be easily accessed by our complimentary shuttles. While you stay with us we will take care of your comforts and needs while you take care of things that matter most.

We offer rooms and services at rates for single occupencies, and we offer discounted packages for multiple rooms when you need reservations for the whole family.
